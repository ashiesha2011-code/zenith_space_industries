A Message from the Founder

My name is Uthkarsh Bhardwaj, and I'm 14 years old. I founded Zenith Space because I believe humanity's greatest chapter hasn't been written yet — and it will be written in the stars.

Zenith Space isn't just about one mission or one structure. It's a full roadmap — from reusable launch vehicles crossing the Kármán Line today, to Habitat One replacing the ISS, to permanent bases on the Moon and Mars, to AstraRing, a 35km rotating megastructure with artificial gravity, and ultimately to Project Eventide — antimatter propulsion toward Proxima Centauri.

Every detail has been designed with purpose. Every mission builds on the last. This is a long game — and Day One starts now.

— Uthkarsh Bhardwaj, Founder & CEO, Zenith Space
